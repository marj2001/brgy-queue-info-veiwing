Privacy Policy

Introduction Welcome to the Barangay San Jose Queuing System. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our queuing system services. Information We Collect We may collect the following types of information: Personal Information: Name, contact details, and any other information you provide when registering or using the service.
Usage Data: Information on how you use our queuing system, including service selections.
Cookies: We may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience and analyze usage patterns. How We Use Your Information We use your information for the following purposes: To manage and operate the queuing system effectively.
To communicate with you regarding your queue status and any updates.
To improve our services and enhance user experience.
To comply with legal obligations and protect the rights and safety of our users. Data Sharing and Disclosure We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.
We may share your information in the following situations: Service Providers: We may engage third-party service providers to assist with our queuing system, who are obligated to
protect your information. Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights or the rights of others. Data Security We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.
However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. Your Rights Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information: The right to access your personal information. The right to request corrections to inaccurate information. The right to request the deletion of your personal information. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new
policy on our website or through direct communication. Contact Us If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our practices, please contact us at: Barangay San Jose
